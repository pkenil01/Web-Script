About Me

Hi, I’m Kenil Patel, an Information Technology student at Ontario Tech University specializing in Technology Management. I’m passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and enjoy learning through hands-on projects. I’m a fast learner, a strong team player, and always willing to take on challenges. I’ve held multiple leadership positions in organizations like Enactus Ontario Tech and the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME), and I’m actively involved in the startup community. In my free time, I enjoy tinkering with my 3D printer and researching new companies and technologies. One day, I hope to launch a startup in the sustainability space to make a positive, long-lasting impact on my community and the world.
